Student Life at Kean

Life at Kean University goes beyond the classrom. We offer a vibran student life experience filled with clubs, organizations, and events that allow students to connect, grow, and make a difference.

Some of the highlights of student life include:

  • Over 130 student clubs and organizations
  • On-campus housing and dining options
  • Recreational sports and fitness facilities
  • Campus events, including concerts, theater performances, and cultural festivals
  • Leadership opportunities through student government and peer mentoring

Kean also provides ample support services to help students succeed academically and personally, such as tutoring, career services, and wellness programs.

Kean University Mission & Values

Kean University is committed to providing a world-class eduaction that prepares students for meaningful careers and lives. We value academic excellence, leadership, equity, and service to the community. Our mission is to foster critical thinking, innocation, and ethical leadership among our students while promoting diversity and inclusion

We believe in empowering students to become lifelong learners and global citizens who are prepared to meet the challenges of an evolving society.

Student Life at Kean

Life at Kean University goes beyond the classrom. We offer a vibran student life experience filled with clubs, organizations, and events that allow students to connect, grow, and make a difference.

Some of the highlights of student life include:

  • Over 130 student clubs and organizations
  • On-campus housing and dining options
  • Recreational sports and fitness facilities
  • Campus events, including concerts, theater performances, and cultural festivals
  • Leadership opportunities through student government and peer mentoring

Kean also provides ample support services to help students succeed academically and personally, such as tutoring, career services, and wellness programs.

Alumni Success

Kean University graduates have gone on to excel in their fields and make a positive impact in their communities. Our alumni network is strong, with graduates working in a variety of industries, including education, business, healthcare, and technology.

Notable alumni include:

  • Carla Harris - Vice Chairman at Morgan Stanley
  • Tommy Franks - Retried U.S. Army General
  • Judy McGrath - Former CEO of MTV Networks

Our graduate are well-equipped to thrive in their careers thanks to Kean's focus on experiential learning, internships, and real-world problem solving.
